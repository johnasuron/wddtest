Turkana Chamber of Commerce

"A hub for economic development and business collaboration in Turkana County"

Site Name

The name Turkana Chamber of Commerce reflects the organization's mission to support local businesses and community development in Turkana County.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to:

  • Act as a platform for economic collaboration and business growth.
  • Provide resources and updates on local events, opportunities, and initiatives.

Scenarios

Potential user questions include:

  • What events can I attend to connect with local businesses?
  • How can I access business resources specific to Turkana County?
  • Where can I learn about partnership opportunities with the chamber?

Color Schema

The website uses the following colors to convey its message:

  • Turquoise (#40E0D0): Accents and highlights, representing Lake Turkana.
  • Warm Sand (#FFD700): Backgrounds, symbolizing the desert landscape.

Typography

The selected fonts for this site are:

  • 'Poppins': Applied to headings for a modern, clean look.
  • 'Roboto': Used for body text for readability and accessibility.
